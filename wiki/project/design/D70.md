# Decision 70 — Remove the in-repo tuning machinery

**Decision.** Every executable and package that existed only to tune prompts leaves the repo; tuning moves wholesale to the external `autotune` tool over the committed data folders of D71. This decision is **structural** — it names exactly what goes and what the tree looks like after; its proof is deterministic phase checks plus the retirement ids D1/D63 own.

Deleted outright:

- `cmd/autotune/`, `cmd/eval-extract/`, `cmd/eval-analysis/` — the driver and both eval runners, with their tests (live and offline).
- `internal/eval/` — config loader, gold loader, scorer, scorecard, acceptance, cache — with its tests.
- `eval/` — the whole data tree (`eval/extract/`, `eval/analysis/`: prompts, `prompt.go` embed shims, configs, improve prompts, READMEs, gold corpora). The production prompts are rehoused into their owning packages **first** (D6/D36), and the gold data is converted into the D71 tune folders **first** — deletion is the last step, and git history retains everything.
- The `Makefile` `eval-extract` build line; any `bin/` artifacts of the deleted commands.
- `internal/llm`'s deprecated compatibility fields (`CallSite.Model`, `CallSite.Temperature`, `CallSite.Reasoning`, `CallSite.MaxTokens`, `DisableReasoning`) — their only consumers were the deleted packages (D63 owns the assertion that the D5 shape is whole again).
- The `github.com/ikigenba/agentkit` require from `go.mod` (D63/D1 own the proofs).

Rewired, not deleted:

- `.envrc` exports `OPENAI_API_KEY` (from `~/.secrets/OPENAI_API_KEY`) replacing the `EVAL_OPENAI_API_KEY` alias — the D72 scorers and the `embed` CLI read the canonical name.
- `.gitignore` drops the `/autotune/` line (the tree becomes committed data, D71) and keeps `/tmp/`; per-folder ignores (`runs/`, `__pycache__/`) live inside each tune folder.
- `AGENTS.md`'s layout section stops naming `eval-extract`, `internal/eval`, and the `eval/` tree, and names `autotune/` as the committed tune-folder workspace.

Kept: `internal/llmtest` (the production packages' test fake — not tuning machinery) and the `tmp/` ignore (still the scratch area).

**Rejected.**
- **Keeping the Go workbench as a second scoring path beside the folders** — two scoring implementations of the same math drift silently; the folder's scorer is the single statement.
- **Deleting the machinery before rehousing the prompts** — `internal/extract` and `internal/ask` embed their prompts *from* `eval/`; deletion first breaks the build. Order is a plan concern, but the dependency is architectural: rehouse, convert data, then delete.
- **Keeping `EVAL_OPENAI_API_KEY`** — an alias that existed only to smuggle the key past ralph's key-hygiene unsetting (research, retired §13); the external tool has no such constraint, and the canonical name is what `embed` and the scorers read.

**Verification.** Pure structure — no ids of its own. Its proof: the D1/D63 retirement ids (R-KDHD-V3XI, R-A3D7-NTLV, R-KFX6-MNEW, R-A4L4-1LCK), a green suite after deletion, and the deleting phase's deterministic greps (no `internal/eval` or `wiki/eval` import anywhere; the named directories absent).
