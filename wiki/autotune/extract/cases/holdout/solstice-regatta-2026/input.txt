The Solstice Regatta finished on June 21, 2026, off Port Fairview. The race drew 87 boats in four classes, and the keelboat class carried a $4,500 first prize. Noor Haddad won the keelboat class by a margin of eleven minutes. Haddad, a ferry captain from Aqaba, was competing in the regatta for the first time.
