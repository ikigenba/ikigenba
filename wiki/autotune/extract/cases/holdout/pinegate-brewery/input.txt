title: Pinegate Brewing opens Duluth taproom
tags: expansion
received on: 2025-11-03

Source text:
Pinegate Brewing, known locally as PGB, opened its second taproom in Duluth in October 2025. The brewery, founded in 2013, distributes to about 90 bars across Minnesota and Wisconsin. PGB said the Duluth taproom will host its barrel-aging program starting next spring.
