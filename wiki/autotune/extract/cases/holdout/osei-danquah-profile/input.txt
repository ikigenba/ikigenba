Source text:
Kwame Osei-Danquah is the head archivist of the Volta Basin Trust. He joined the trust in 2016 after twelve years cataloguing colonial-era survey maps at the National Records Office. Osei-Danquah studied history at the University of Ghana and has published two reference guides on watershed cartography. He is based in Tamale.
