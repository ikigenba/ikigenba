title: Arden Mills H1 2026 results
tags: finance, results
received on: 2026-07-30

Source text:
Arden Mills reported first-half 2026 revenue of $31.6 million, up from $27.9 million in the second half of 2025. First-half operating profit was $4.8 million, against $3.5 million in the prior half. The company commissioned two new grain silos during the period, bringing its storage capacity to 64,000 tonnes.
