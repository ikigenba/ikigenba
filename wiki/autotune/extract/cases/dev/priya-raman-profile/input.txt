Priya Raman is the chief technology officer of Halewood Systems. She joined the company in 2019 after eleven years at Corvex Labs, where she led the compiler tools group. Raman studied electrical engineering at the University of Waterloo and holds four patents in program analysis. She lives in Ottawa.
