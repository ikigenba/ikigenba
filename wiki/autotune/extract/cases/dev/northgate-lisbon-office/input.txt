title: Northgate Analytics opens Lisbon office
tags: expansion
received on: 2024-07-02

Source text:
Northgate Analytics, often shortened to NGA in industry press, opened its first European office in Lisbon in June 2024. The company, headquartered in Toronto, employs about 240 people and sells forecasting software to utility operators. NGA said the Lisbon office will focus on customers in Portugal and Spain.
