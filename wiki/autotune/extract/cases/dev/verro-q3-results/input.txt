Verro Logistics reported third-quarter 2026 revenue of $18.4 million, up from $15.1 million in the second quarter. Third-quarter net income was $2.2 million, against $1.9 million in the second quarter. The company opened three new depots during the third quarter, bringing its network to 27 locations.
