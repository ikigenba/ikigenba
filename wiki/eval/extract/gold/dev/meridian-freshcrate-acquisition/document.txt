Meridian Foods, a regional grocery cooperative founded in 1998, acquired the meal-kit startup FreshCrate in March 2025 for $40 million. FreshCrate's founder, Dana Okafor, will join Meridian as head of its new Prepared Meals division. The acquisition gives Meridian access to FreshCrate's cold-chain logistics network, which delivers refrigerated kits to 12 metropolitan areas. Analysts said the deal reflects a broader shift toward direct-to-consumer grocery models, though Meridian will keep the FreshCrate brand independent for at least two years.
