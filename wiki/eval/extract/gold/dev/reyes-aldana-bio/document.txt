Tomas Reyes-Aldana was born in Valparaíso in 1958. Reyes-Aldana trained as a marine painter before turning to printmaking in the late 1980s. His woodcut series "Nine Harbors" won the Almeida Prize in 1994. Reyes-Aldana taught at the Catholic University of Valparaíso for two decades and retired in 2019.
